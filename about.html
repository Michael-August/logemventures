About Us

Longem International Ventures Ltd. is a procurement and Workforce supply  business based in Lagos, the business hub of West Africa. We offer a streamlined and synchronised solution to fulfill all our clients needs. Understanding that quality of service does make a difference, we bring to the fore a simplistic solution for our clients by taking the time to develop a thorough understanding of their business and needs. Our commitment to service excellence is exemplified with the forethought of our “clients, suppliers and staff are our business”. Complete client satisfaction is the key driving factor behind the excellence in service that we deliver.

Our Vision

To help organizations achieve their goals and objectives by supplying them highly skilled and experienced professionals and delivering impeccable procurement services.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering the highest quality service and building customer loyalty....through commitment, integrity, collaboration and a drive for continual improvement.

Our Core Values

Character

We are always guided by our ethics and ideals in our journey to achieving our goals. These ethics are demonstrated in the way we do our business as we believe the best relationships are built on integrity and trust.

Commitment

We are always committed to delivering quality service and this drives us as an organization, ensuring operational excellence and continual improvement.

Collaboration

LWe believe that we can only succeed by working and learning together,  growing and leveraging on the synergy of team work.

Care, Health and Safety

We care about our people, customers and environment. This care is expressed by ensuring safety and well being of our personnel and customers, consideration and preservationof our environment.
