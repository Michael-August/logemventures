Our Core Services

Workforce Supply

As a workforce supply company, we deal with professionals on a daily basis sourcing the best skills and competence  for our clients by careful assessment of personnel to ensure our cleints requirements are met and exceeded. We provide our clients with the assurance of quality service and a results orientated attitude.

Procurement

Longem International Ventures Ltd. offers an extensive range of commercial, industrial and technical procurement and supply services. We provide industrial machinery equipment, oil drilling and production supplies, industrial electrical & electronic systems, labouratory equipment, office stationaries, personal protection equipment, specialty items, spare parts, as well as manufacturing, construction as well as operating consumables for the industries that we service. Included in these services are comprehensive support logistics, delivery, installation and training solutions.

About Us

Longem International Ventures Ltd. is a procurement and Workforce supply  business based in Lagos, the business hub of West Africa. We offer a streamlined and synchronised solution to fulfill all our clients needs. Understanding that quality of service does make a difference, we bring to the fore a simplistic solution for our clients by taking the time to develop a thorough understanding of their business and needs. Our commitment to service excellence is exemplified with the forethought of our “clients, suppliers and staff are our business”. Complete client satisfaction is the key driving factor behind the excellence in service that we deliver.
